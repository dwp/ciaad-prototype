Bereavement Support Payment

Potentially eligible

The caller is potentially eligible because they are:

  • working age
  • married or in a civil partnership

What they could get

They could get:

  • a £2500 lump sum
  • up to 18 monthly payments of £100

The payments could be higher if they have dependent children.

The money is tax free and paid directly into their bank account.

Bereavement Support Payment will not affect any benefits for a year after the first payment.
